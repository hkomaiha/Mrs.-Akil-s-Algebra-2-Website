Classroom Policies

Late Work

All work must be turned in on time to be eligible for full credit. If late, it will be scored as follows:

  • 1 day late → 90% credit
  • 2-3 days late → 70% credit
  • 4-7 days late → 25% credit
  • More than 7 days late → No credit

Grading Policy

Your grade is based on two categories:

  • Assessments: 80%
  • Classwork & Assignments: 20%

Retakes are allowed if the following conditions are met:

  • All prior assignments are complete and on time
  • You schedule the retake before or after school
  • You submit a preparation form

Note: Retake scores replace the original - even if they are lower. No retakes after the unit ends.

Cell Phones & AirPods

All students must follow the classroom technology expectations:

  • Phones must be placed in the phone holder before the bell rings
  • AirPods are not allowed unless you're watching a video lesson
  • Violations result in office pickup or suspension

Academic Honesty

Cheating, copying, or using unauthorized resources is not allowed. This includes:

  • Sharing answers, sending photos, or using AI to complete tests
  • Copying from another student or an online source

Consequences may include a zero, loss of retake opportunity, and a dishonesty note on your report card.

Attendance & Make-Up Work

Students are expected to attend daily and stay on pace with make-up work:

  • Make-up work is due within the number of days missed
  • Assessments must be made up within one week
  • Testing is available Thursdays or by appointment

Hallway Passes

Passes are for emergencies only. The following guidelines apply:

  • Submit E-Pass using your Chromebook
  • Teacher approval is required before leaving
  • Check back in when returning to class

How to Succeed

To be successful in Algebra 2, follow these habits:

  • Be on time and prepared every day
  • Watch video notes before class and complete guided notes
  • Participate fully and submit all assignments
  • Ask questions and communicate early
