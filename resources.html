Class Resources

Google Classroom

Google Classroom is the central hub for everything we do in Algebra 2.

All assignments - whether it’s daily classwork, homework, or assessments - will be posted and submitted through Google Classroom. Students should get into the habit of checking the Classroom page every day for updates, materials, and announcements.


If you're ever unsure what’s going on in class - start with Google Classroom.

DeltaMath

DeltaMath is the primary platform for online math practice and homework assignments.

Students will complete many of their assigned Algebra 2 practice sets through DeltaMath. Each problem is automatically graded and provides instant feedback. Unlike paper homework, DeltaMath allows multiple attempts per question, giving students the opportunity to truly understand the concepts by trying again. Even after they complete their assignments, DeltaMath offers near unlimited problems for them to keep working on if they want.


If you're struggling or preparing for a quiz, DeltaMath is a great place to review and build mastery.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a learning platform that covers every Algebra 2 topic you'll learn.

Students are encouraged to use Khan Academy when they want a fresh explanation or a second voice to explain a topic. Sometimes hearing a lesson explained differently is all it takes for something to click. If a concept feels confusing after class or after trying DeltaMath, Khan Academy can help reinforce your understanding.


Think of Khan Academy as your always-available tutor - ready to explain anything from factoring to logarithms, 24/7.
